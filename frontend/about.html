Common Questions

What is the delivery area?expand_more

We deliver within a 10KM radius of Padikkal, Velimukku, Malappuram.

How is the delivery fee calculated?expand_more

Free delivery for your first order and all orders above ₹500. For other orders, a distance-based fee (₹15 base + ₹8/km) applies.

Can I pay with cash?expand_more

Yes! We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (GPay, PhonePe), and Credit/Debit Cards.

How do I cancel or modify my order?expand_more

Contact us on WhatsApp immediately after placing the order. Orders can be cancelled within 10 minutes of placement.
